Connect Cloud Sync
To share the draft setup across devices, the app uses JSONBin.io — a free cloud storage service. Takes 2 minutes to set up.
1. Go to jsonbin.io → Sign up free
2. In the dashboard, click API Keys → copy your Master Key
3. Paste it below and click Save — the app creates a bin automatically
4. Share your GitHub Pages link with your league — everyone sees the same data ✓
🔒 Your key is stored only in your browser. Share the Master Key with your league commissioner so they can update the draft too, or keep it to yourself so only you can edit.
